Bom Jesus de Braga

The event will take place in Braga, one of the oldest portuguese cities, established in Roman times as Bracara Avgvsta. The venue will be Bom Jesus de Braga, a tourist place with a beautiful park and a 19th century hydraulical elevator which is one the oldest of its kind still functioning in the world.
