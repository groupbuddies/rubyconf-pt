Bom Jesus de Braga

Bom Jesus is a privileged spot in Braga, sitting on top of the mountains and surrounded by hauntingly beautiful gardens and a peaceful lake, that turn it into one of the most relaxed and zen places that you can find in Portugal.

How to get here?*

From the airport

The closest airport is the OPO (Porto), 30 minutes away from Braga. You can catch a direct bus right at the airport, with a service called Get Bus.

From Braga

From the city to Bom Jesus, there’s public transports leaving every 30 minutes. You can see the scheduling here.
